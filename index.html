Callistemon

A collection of tools and demos leveraging HL7 FHIR and SNOMED CT.

Currently focused on Sparked eRquesting IG https://build.fhir.org/ig/hl7au/au-fhir-erequesting/

Available toys

eRequest Filler Reception

A demonstration patient reception app that allows staff to quickly look up a presenting patient by name or QR code, and view any associated requests submitted by their provider.

eRequest Dashboard

An interactive dashboard for exploring service requests with advanced filters powered by SNOMED CT and FHIR terminology services. The dashboard updates in real time as new requests arrive and existing ones change.

eRequest Placer

A tool demonstrating electronic request workflows and the practical use of FHIR and SNOMED CT. Users can switch between two clinician roles (each with their own favourites list), add coded clinical notes, and create both Pathology and Imaging requests.

Data Generators

A collection of scripts and utilities to generate test datasets for FHIR and SNOMED CT experimentation.
